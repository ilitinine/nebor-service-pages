The CRM enrichment and hygiene playbook

What is a CRM enrichment and hygiene workflow?

A CRM enrichment and hygiene workflow keeps every record in your CRM complete, deduplicated, validated, and current, automatically. Instead of a database that decays the moment data goes in, you get a living source of truth your team actually trusts.

Here is the problem. Most CRMs fail not from lack of effort, but because they are static by default. Data pours in from forms, imports, outbound lists, trade shows, and manual entry, and nothing keeps it clean.

So you end up with contacts who left three years ago, emails that bounce, duplicate records that confuse reps, and fields that are empty or wrong. Your team stops trusting it and keeps their real contacts in spreadsheets.

This workflow fixes that at the root. Clay connects to your CRM and works every record: it audits what is missing, merges duplicates, enriches the gaps from many providers, validates every email and phone, and catches contacts the moment they change jobs.

Then it unifies the silos, so a change in one place updates everywhere, and keeps running on a schedule and on triggers, so the CRM stays clean without anyone doing data entry.

It runs inside your own stack, so the clean, living CRM it builds, and everything it enables, stays yours to keep.

What a dirty CRM costs you

The graveyard, brought back to life

These are the problems a static CRM creates, and what the workflow does about each one.

The mess

Contacts who already left

A large share of your contacts changed jobs since they went in. The workflow catches the move, updates the company and the contact, and keeps the relationship instead of emailing a dead address.

Emails that bounce

Dead addresses quietly wreck your deliverability and your sender reputation. Every email is verified across multiple checkers, and anything that fails is pruned before it ever sends.

Duplicate records

The same company sits in your CRM three times, worked by two reps who do not know about each other. Duplicates are matched and merged into one clean record.

Empty and wrong fields

The cold email that opens with "Hi {FIRST_NAME}" because the field was blank. Enrichment fills the gaps from many providers, so every record is complete enough to act on.

Data trapped in silos

Support knows the complaint, marketing knows the campaign, sales knows the promise, and none of it talks. The workflow unifies it into one record every team can see.

A CRM nobody trusts

When the data is bad, reps give up and keep their real contacts in spreadsheets and inboxes. A clean, living CRM is one they actually use.

Bad data does not just sit there quietly. It costs you deals, deliverability, and the trust of your own team.

FAQ
What is a CRM enrichment and hygiene workflow?

An automated system that keeps every record in your CRM complete, deduplicated, validated, and current. Clay connects to your CRM and continuously audits, enriches, and cleans the data, so it stays a source of truth instead of decaying into a graveyard.

Why does my CRM get dirty in the first place?

Because CRMs are static by default. Data comes in from forms, imports, outbound lists, and manual entry, and nothing keeps it fresh. Contacts change jobs, emails bounce, duplicates pile up, and fields go empty. Without automation, once bad data is in, it stays.

What does the enrichment fill in?

Firmographics like company size, industry, and funding, the tech stack a company runs, verified work emails and phone numbers, and social profiles. It runs as a waterfall across many providers, so coverage is far higher than any single tool gives.

How do you handle duplicates and dead records?

Duplicate records are merged into one clean record, so reps stop working the same account twice. Emails and phones are validated, and contacts that are truly dead or unverifiable are archived, so your active database is only real, reachable people.

How does it stay current?

It runs continuously, on a schedule and on triggers. Clay watches for job changes, funding, and other events, and updates the record the moment they happen, so the data does not drift back out of date.

Does it work with my CRM?

Yes. It works with HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive, and unifies the data so a change in one place is reflected everywhere. It connects to your enrichment and validation tools through Clay and n8n.

Do I own the system?

Yes. It is built inside your own CRM and tools, so the clean, living database it creates stays yours. There is no platform to rent, and nothing breaks if you ever part ways with us.
