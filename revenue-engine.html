FAQ

Things teams ask before they start.

How is this different from a lead generation agency? +
Lead-gen agencies hand you a list. We build the systems inside your company · the data backbone, workflows, and integrations your team owns and operates. When we're done, the infrastructure stays with you and keeps compounding.
How long before we see results? +
Most clients see their first qualified pipeline within 0–7 weeks of launch. Building a sustainable GTM system isn't overnight · the clients who get the best results treat it as infrastructure, not a quick fix.
What if we already have a CRM and tools in place? +
Better. We work with what you have and optimize the stack. We don't rip and replace · we integrate into your existing ecosystem and build workflows around your current setup.
Who's this for? +
B2B teams with a clear value proposition who want to scale outbound, improve inbound quality, or clean up GTM operations. Our best clients compare us to hiring a sales rep, not to buying a tool.
What does pricing look like? +
Implementation packages start with a one-time setup fee plus monthly monitoring. Exact scope depends on whether you need the full GTM stack or specific workflows. Book a call and we'll scope it.
Do you replace our SDR / AE team? +
No. We build the infrastructure your reps run on. Reps still own conversations, demos, and closing · they just stop spending half their day on research, data entry, and chasing routing rules. The system does the unsexy work; the team does the selling.
What tools do you typically wire together? +
Whatever your stack already has, plus a few proven additions. Most builds touch HubSpot or Salesforce as the core, Apollo or Clay for data, Smartlead or Instantly for outbound, n8n or Zapier for orchestration, and a layer of LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic) for research and personalization.
How involved does our team need to be? +
We need 2–4 hours a week of one ops or RevOps lead during build, plus access to your CRM and key tools. After handoff, the system is operated by your team · we document everything and train the people who'll keep it running.
What happens after the engagement ends? +
You own everything. The workflows live inside your stack, your reps operate them, and the documentation is yours. Most teams keep us on a light retainer for monthly tuning and new layers · but you're never locked in.
