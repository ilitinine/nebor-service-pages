How we work

An engagement
that compounds.

No 80-page deck handoff. We diagnose, build inside your stack, embed with your team, and the system gets sharper the longer it runs.

Week 1–2

Diagnose

Audit your data, stack, and sales motion. Identify the highest-leverage workflows to wire first.

You'll see
  • A written diagnostic of where pipeline is leaking right now
  • A ranked list of 3–5 workflows we'd build first, with expected impact
  • A clear scope, timeline, and what success looks like at week 7
What we'll need
  • Read access to your CRM and outbound tools
  • One ops/RevOps lead, ~3 hours that fortnight
  • Last 6 months of pipeline + win-loss data
2
Week 3–6

Build

Inside your stack, end to end. Workflows, integrations, and AI pieces engineered to your motion.

You'll see
  • Workflows going live one-by-one inside your own CRM
  • Weekly demo of what shipped + what's next
  • The first qualified pipeline come through the new system
What we'll need
  • Admin access to your CRM and the tools we wire together
  • 2–4 hours/week from a sales lead for QA on the outbound voice
  • Approval on a small monthly tool budget (~€500–€1,500)
3
Week 6–9

Embed

Train your team to operate it. Documentation, runbooks, and the handoff so the system stays with you.

You'll see
  • Screen-recorded runbooks for every workflow your team will operate
  • A 60-minute live training with your ops + sales leads
  • The system fully owned by your team, with us on standby
What we'll need
  • 2 hours from each rep/operator for the live training session
  • One ops lead designated as the long-term owner
  • Sign-off on the documented runbook library

Monthly tuning, model retraining, and new workflow layers as your motion evolves. The engine improves with use.

You'll see
  • Monthly performance review · what's working, what to retire

  • New workflow layers added as your motion expands
What we'll need
  • 30–60 min monthly review with the RevOps lead
  • Ongoing access to read CRM signals + reply data

Questions

Questions we get asked
How is Nebor different from a lead generation agency? +−
Lead-gen agencies hand you a list. We build the systems inside your company · the data backbone, workflows, and integrations your team owns and operates. When we're done, the infrastructure stays with you and keeps compounding.
How long before we see results? +−
Most clients see their first qualified pipeline within 0-7 weeks of launch. But we're honest: building a sustainable GTM system isn't overnight work. The clients who get the best results understand this is an investment in infrastructure that compounds.
What if we already have a CRM and some tools in place? +−
Better. We work with what you have and optimize the stack. We don't rip and replace · we integrate into your existing ecosystem and build workflows around your current setup.
Who is this for? +−
B2B teams with a clear value proposition who want to scale outbound, improve inbound quality, or clean up GTM operations. Our best clients compare us to hiring a sales rep instead of buying a tool.
What does pricing look like? +−
Implementation packages start with a one-time setup fee plus monthly monitoring. Exact scope depends on whether you need the full GTM stack or specific workflows. Book a call and we'll scope it.
What tools do you work with? +−
Whatever your stack already has. Most builds sit on HubSpot or Salesforce as the core, with Clay orchestrating data and enrichment and a layer of AI (Claude) for research and personalization.
