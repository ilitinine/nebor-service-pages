The Job Search

Questions

Questions we get asked
How long before we see results? +−
The build runs in phases: diagnose in the first two weeks, workflows going live inside your CRM from week three, your team trained and owning it by week nine. The first cleaned records and live automations land early, and the foundation compounds from there.
What happens to our data during a migration? +−
Nothing gets lost. Every record, deal and touchpoint is reconciled against the source system before we cut over, and you get the reconciliation report. And we only migrate when consolidation genuinely calls for it: most engagements fix the instance you already have.
Will our team really use it? +−
That is what the embed phase is for. Every workflow ships with a screen-recorded runbook, your ops and sales leads get live training, and one person on your side is designated as the owner. The system ends up fully yours to run, with us on standby.
What do we need to give you access to? +−
Read access to your CRM and outbound tools for the diagnostic, admin access when we wire integrations, and a few hours a week from one ops or RevOps lead. The exact list is spelled out per phase before each phase starts.
Do we have to rip out our current CRM? +−
No. Most engagements fix and extend the instance you already have: restructure, clean, automate. A migration only happens when you're consolidating tools or the current setup is beyond repair, and then nothing gets lost on the way over.
Can you work with a non-HubSpot stack? +−
Yes. HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive as the core, and we wire the rest of your stack into it. As long as the CRM has a reasonable API and admin access, the same playbook applies.
