The Job Search

Questions

Questions we get asked
What's different about how you do ABM? +−
Most ABM is a target list and a budget. We connect the whole motion: identification, enrichment, scoring, dynamic creative, and rep activation, all orchestrated so the accounts that matter get a coordinated push across every channel instead of the same ad as everyone else.
How do you tie campaigns back to revenue? +−
Every touch is stamped with the source, signal, and sequence that produced it, and the CRM is the single source of truth. Paid, organic, and outbound all roll up to the same pipeline number, so you see exactly what created pipeline.
What if our website isn't built for this? +−
Dynamic landing pages, smart forms, and visitor identification slot in without a rebuild. We work with Webflow, Framer, or your existing CMS, and we'll flag it early if the site is the bottleneck rather than the engine.
How fast can inbound leads be routed? +−
Form-fill to the rep's Slack is typically under 90 seconds. Enrichment, scoring, and round-robin routing happen on write, the moment the form lands, so nobody who was interested goes cold waiting.
Do you build the creative for paid and ABM ads? +−
We build the audience, attribution, and routing systems, and collaborate with your in-house or agency team on ad copy and landing-page variants. Either way, every variant is tied back to the pipeline it produced.
Can you work with a non-HubSpot stack? +−
Yes. We build on your CRM as the core, whether that's HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive, with Clay orchestrating data and enrichment, LinkedIn and the ad platforms for reach, and a layer of AI for research and personalization. As long as the CRM has a reasonable API and admin access, the same playbook applies.
